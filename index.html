Welcome to the history of vaccination

What is Vaccination?

Vaccination is a biological preparation containing a partial killed form of a pathogen(such as a virus or bacteria). This toxin as been injected in the body for the immune system to remember the pathogen, allowing immune system to work rapidly when it come across the actual pathogen.

Varriolation

Varriolation is the process of exposing healty individuals to small pox scabs and compox scabs to provide immunity to small pox. However, 1-3% individual died in the process, this type of varriolation was famous in asian countries back in early 1400 to late 1700.

First Vaccination

In the year 1796 the first vaccination of small pox was created by british physician Edward Jenner
